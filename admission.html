ADMISSION RULES AND REGULATION

  1. Admission to various classes is based on the availability of seats.
  2. Birth certificate must be produced while seeking admissions to KG-1/ KG-2/KG-3 & Class-1
  3. New Entrance from class-II to X must submit original transfer certificate issued by the previous school during admission.
  4. Candidates seeking admission from other institutions should take up an entrance exam conducted by the school.
  5. The prescribed fees once paid will not be refunded under any circumstances.
  6. Students who are under different caste wise category should produce caste certificate issued by the Tahsildar during admission along with application.
  7. All necessary documentation and other necessary submission asked for must be done to complete enrollment formalities.
